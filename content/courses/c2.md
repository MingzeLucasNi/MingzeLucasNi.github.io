## Course Outline

This course is taught in two big part. In the first half, the class reviews the fundamental economic theory for policy analysis and familiarize students with quantitative tools (econometrics, input-output analysis, system modelling, optimization) and qualitative tools (SWOT, analytical hierarchy process, analytical network process, Delphi method). In the second half, students are required to write a policy analysis paper and the class time is used for focus group discussion with invited speaker. Past speaker are important figures in Indonesia energy sector including government official and private sector
